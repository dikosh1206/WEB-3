About Morning Crust

Our Story

Morning Crust is your cozy spot for fresh pastries and aromatic coffee. We believe in using the finest ingredients and baking every item with love. Whether you come for breakfast or a sweet treat — every bite feels like home.

Founded by Amanzhol Aldiyar and Kalzhanov Zhansultan, the café is inspired by European-style bakeries, offering warm vibes and an unforgettable aroma of morning coffee.
